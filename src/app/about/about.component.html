About Me

My exposure to ETL pipelines, data integration, and real-time event processing makes me well-suited for both backend and data-intensive platforms.

I hold two Microsoft certifications: Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900) and Azure Data Fundamentals (DP-900). These credentials reflect my strong grounding in cloud architecture, data services, and enterprise-grade deployments on Azure.

In my professional journey, I’ve played the roles of contributor, lead, and mentor.
